Minha carreira foi construída sobre o ecossistema **.NET** — do Web Forms e MVC ao .NET Core — com passagens por arquiteturas DDD, microsserviços, Flutter, Angular e implantação na AWS. Hoje, as stacks centrais do meu dia a dia são **AWS, Python, Java e IA/ML**.

Sou graduado em Sistemas para Internet pela UNIT (Universidade Tiradentes) e mestrando em Ciência da Computação na UFBA (Universidade Federal da Bahia), com foco de pesquisa em Análise e Visualização de Dados. Tenho sólida experiência em engenharia de software e métodos ágeis — Scrum, Kanban, modelagem de sistemas e especificação de requisitos.

Durante a graduação, fui voluntário na Mozilla e Microsoft Student Partner (MSP), organizando eventos e ministrando oficinas de desenvolvimento.

## English (EN)

Hello! I'm **Rafael Tavares**, a software engineer with over a decade of experience in software development, technical leadership, and legacy system modernization.

I currently work as an **AI Tech Lead** at [Zup](https://zupinnovation.com/), an [Itaú](https://www.itau.com.br/) company, based in Aracaju, Brazil. I lead a squad of eight developers migrating legacy applications built in COBOL and .NET Framework 3.5 — from on-premises environments to modern architectures on AWS, using Python, Java, and generative AI tools such as StackSpot, AWS Q Transform, and Kiro.

My journey at Zup began as a Senior Developer on a billing project for Recovery. I then worked as a Senior Back-end Developer with .NET Core and Kotlin on **iti**, Itaú's banking app, building highly available and resilient microservices — from pipeline creation to production observability. Over the past six years, I have also contributed to modernization initiatives within Itaú's Deprecation program as a Full Stack Developer using Angular, .NET Core, and AWS cloud services.

Before Zup, I coordinated the development team at **Serveloja**, where I was responsible for all production deliveries, reporting directly to the CEO and working with stakeholders to evolve payment management products for card terminals. That experience strengthened my role as a technical leader and Scrum Master, while deepening my expertise in C#, Angular, React, SQL Server, and Azure.

My career has been built on the **.NET** ecosystem — from Web Forms and MVC to .NET Core — with experience in DDD architectures, microservices, Flutter, Angular, and AWS deployments. Today, my core day-to-day stacks are **AWS, Python, Java, and AI/ML**.

I hold a degree in Internet Systems from UNIT (Universidade Tiradentes) and I am pursuing a Master's degree in Computer Science at UFBA (Federal University of Bahia), with a research focus on Data Analysis and Visualization. I have solid experience in software engineering practices and agile methods — Scrum, Kanban, system modeling, and requirements specification.

During my undergraduate studies, I volunteered at Mozilla and served as a Microsoft Student Partner (MSP), organizing events and teaching development workshops.
